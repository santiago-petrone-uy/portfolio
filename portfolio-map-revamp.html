Map Feature Revamp Motion + UX

The client

The Sandbox is a community-driven platform where creators can monetize virtual lands, voxel assets & gaming experiences on the blockchain.

The project

Map scanning didn't help customers find clear land information. The lack of a filtering system made locating what they were looking for hard. Land details were often cluttered or irrelevant, making it tough to understand the land value.

Design Approach

We researched, analyzed, and mapped previous customer experiences to improve the filter system and land details page. We then brainstormed, prototyped, and tested potential solutions with customers. Based on the test results, we selected the best solution to be implemented by the devs.
