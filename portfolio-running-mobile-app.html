Running Mobile App Motion + UI

The client

ePlay Digital Inc. is an award-winning real-time 3D (RT3D) and Augmented Reality (AR) sports content and game creator, as well as a metaverse publisher.

The project

We take care of creating a cohesive motion experience for the Klocked app. Motion is crucial for creating a good experience, making the app more engaging, intuitive, and enjoyable to use.

Motion Approach

The motion approach focuses on enhancing user experience through smooth transitions, feedback for actions, visual hierarchy, microinteractions, contextual animations, and a consistent motion language.

Conclusions

We were able to improve guidance, establish a visual hierarchy, and create a consistent motion language thanks to the contextual animations and microinteractions.
